Sharepic generator

Welcome to the Rheinos-and-RheinIIs-Social-Media-Sharepic-Generator-Overview-Page

I couldn't make this word any longer. Here's an overview over out templates :)

Standard Post

Template for everything that hasn't a template. Use this for the post after a (fantasy) tournament or after a training camp or everything else. If you use it for the post after a non-fantasy tournament, you can put score posts behind this in the post!

Score Post

Template to post the result of a game after a game. Please don't make a post for a single group stage game! You either put all results in a single post with multiple pictures or just put the result in the story after a game.

Schedule Post

Template to announce league gamedays and other events with a schedule (e.g. tournament group stages). If there are more than five games scheduled, split the schedule on multiple pictures, which should be posted in a single post. You can leave out games without one of our teams too! But please don't use this template to announce a single game (e.g. a knockout stage game)! You can use the announcement template instead!

Announcement Post

Template for all kinds of announcements. Whenever there are great news or just normal news to spread, use this template, e.g. when we're about play a game in knockout stage. If you use this template to post about a game (e.g. to say that we're playing the quarter finals vs Team X at Y o'clock in the livestream), please use this as announcement only (prior to game) and use the score template for results. Also please don't announce group stage games :)
